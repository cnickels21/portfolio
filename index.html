About Me

I grew up in a small town in northern Wisconsin where my parents own a bar and motel. That coupled with my tech school coursework gives me a foundation in business management and accounting. I have been working in the service industry most of my life, but that had grown stagnant for me. That is why I have commited myself to software development, specifically because, in this technological era, I saw an opportunity to become a part of an innovative and prosperous future. My years in the service industry have gifted me with a calm and level head. This allows me to look at the problem and find the best path to a solution calmy and effectively. Also, with the experience of working with so many different people, I have developed a personality that allows me to build a connection with most anyone, making me a valuable asset on a diverse team. I am looking for a way to help the world continue to grow and to continue growing along with it.
